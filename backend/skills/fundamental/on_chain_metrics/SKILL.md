## Exchange Inflows / Outflows
- **Large inflows to exchanges** = selling pressure imminent (bearish).
- **Large outflows from exchanges** = holders moving to cold storage (bullish / accumulation).
- Look at 7-day moving average, not daily noise.

## MVRV Ratio (Market Value / Realized Value)
| MVRV | Interpretation |
|------|---------------|
| < 1.0 | Capitulation — historically strong buy zone |
| 1.0 – 2.0 | Fair value — mixed signals |
| 2.0 – 3.5 | Overvalued — take profits on longs |
| > 3.5 | Euphoria — high probability top |

## SOPR (Spent Output Profit Ratio)
- **SOPR > 1** and rising = profit-taking, potential top.
- **SOPR < 1** and falling = panic selling, potential bottom.
- Reset below 1 followed by reclaim = bullish reversal.

## Funding Rates
- Positive funding > 0.01% / 8h = longs paying shorts, crowded long (caution).
- Negative funding < -0.01% / 8h = shorts paying longs, crowded short (potential squeeze).
