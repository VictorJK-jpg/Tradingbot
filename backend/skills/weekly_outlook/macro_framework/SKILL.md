## Checklist — Every Monday Before Markets Open
1. **Futures & Funding**: Are crypto perps at extreme premiums/discounts?
2. **DXY (Dollar Index)**: Strength above 105 historically caps risk assets.
3. **US 10Y Treasury**: Yields > 4.5% reduce crypto risk appetite.
4. **VIX**: Spikes > 25 = correlations rise to 1, reduce altcoin exposure.
5. **Bitcoin Dominance (BTC.D)**:
   - Rising BTC.D = risk-off in crypto, altcoins underperform.
   - Falling BTC.D = risk-on, altseason potential.
6. **ETF Flows**: Net negative for 3+ days = institutional cooling.
7. **Geopolitical**: Any active conflict with energy-market impact? Oil spikes hurt risk assets.
